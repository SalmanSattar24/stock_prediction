## 💰 MONEY AT RISK

Using the example above:
- You risk: $152.50 - $140.30 = **$12.20 per share**
- 12 shares × $12.20 = **$146.40 total risk** (1.5% of $100k)
- If stop is hit, you lose $146.40
- If target is hit, you gain $275.40

### Scenario A: STOP LOSS HIT

Result: LOSS of $146.40 (TRADE CLOSED)
```

### Scenario B: TAKE PROFIT HIT
```
Price rises to $175.38
↓
Automatic SELL order executes
↓
